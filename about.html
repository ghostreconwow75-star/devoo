About AICodeKit

Free AI-powered developer tools built for developers who ship fast.

AICodeKit was created to solve a simple problem: developers need powerful tools, but most are either paid, require signup, or track your data.

We believe in:

  • 🚀 Speed — Tools that work instantly, no loading screens
  • 🔒 Privacy — Your code never leaves your browser unless you choose AI features
  • 💰 Free — No paywalls, no "premium tiers," no credit cards
  • 🛠️ Quality — Tools that actually solve real problems

Built with modern web technologies and AI APIs. We don't store your code, your data, or your personal information.
