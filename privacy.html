Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

1. Information We Don't Collect

AICodeKit is designed with privacy first. We do not store, log, or retain any code you paste into our tools. All processing happens in your browser or via secure API calls that are not persisted.

2. Information We Do Collect

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device type and browser information
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Referral sources

This data is anonymized and cannot personally identify you.

3. Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies via Google Analytics. You can disable analytics cookies in your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google's Gemini API for AI-powered features. Code sent for AI analysis is processed by Google's servers subject to their privacy policy. We do not retain this data.

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email privacy@aicodekit.com.
